function:roll A:n plus B:n{if A<=2{result:0}result:A+B}

STR_A:5 DEX_A:5 CON_A:5 INT_A:2 WIS_A:2 P_A:2 ARMOR:2
WS_A:WIS_A+P_A ES_A:CON_A+P_A

STR_B:3 DEX_B:3 CON_B:1 INT_B:2 WIS_B:3 P_B:2
WS_B:WIS_B+P_B ES_B:CON_B+P_B

WOUND_CHANCE:[count {(ES_B+1)..(STR_A+ES_B)} in STR_Ad(STR_A+ES_B)]
