\ VARIABLES \

\ An enemy group traveling this many hours: \
GROUP:    5
HOURS:    6

\ ...Has to make a Wisdom (Survival) check each hour of travel: \
SURVIVAL: 1d20 + 6 \ (and disadvantage if moving at double pace) \
SURVIVDC: 15

\ ...Or be attacked by a number of winter wolves (or similar) based on the size of the group: \
WOLVES:   2d6
MINIMUM:  1
CHUNK:    4d12

\ Which deal about this much damage each, minimum per encounter: \
DAMAGE: ((2d6 + 4) + (4d8 + 2d8))

\ ...Up to a maximum number of times per day: \
TIMES:   1


\ PROGRAM \

output ([lowest of TIMES and HOURS d (SURVIVAL < SURVIVDC)]) d ([highest of (GROUP * WOLVES) / CHUNK and MINIMUM]) named "attacked by gd wolves!"

output DAMAGE * ([lowest of TIMES and HOURS d (SURVIVAL < SURVIVDC)]) d ([highest of (GROUP * WOLVES) / CHUNK and MINIMUM]) / GROUP named "distribute (at least) this much damage!"
