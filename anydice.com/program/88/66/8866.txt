\It seems that +1 & -1 dif give approximately 17% bonus to success or failure, respectively\
\Each consecutively higher or lower dif gives 3% less change from the last dif... perhaps because the explosion ins't improving?\
\Let's assume that each dif will cause around 15% change\
\1 Dif = ~15% change\
\2 Dif = ~30% change\
\3 Dif = ~45% change\
\4 Dif = ~60% change\
\5 Dif = ~75% change\
\We can assume that +- 4 total dif almost guarantees success or failure, and dif past that is redundant (only slightly increases chances of success)\
\Note the effect of character traits... Limited Attribute + Novice Base Skill (-3) puts character at 45% greater chance of failure, very difficult
while Exceptional Attribute + Master Specialized Skill (+3) gives 45% greater chance of success, almost guaranteed\
\This means that action that use weak traits are very difficult while good traits are easy... this is good\
\This also means that dif 0 for good traits is at 11  (difficult action) while dif 0 for weak traits is 5 (easy action.) this is appropriate\
