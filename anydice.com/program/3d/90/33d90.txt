\ interpolation between 2@3d6 and 1@3d6 
  by swapping out white dice for red dice @ 1/blood token spent
  result is the highest of the middle value and the highest red die \

function: middle X:n and Y:n and Z:n {
result: 2@[sort {X, Y, Z}] }


output [middle d6 and d6 and d6] named "3d6"
output [middle d6 and d6 and d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7}] named "2d6 + 1d10"
output [middle d6 and d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7} and d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7}] named "1d6 + 2d10"
output [middle d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7} and d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7} and d{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,7,7,7}] named "3d10"
