function: with P:n proficiency and N:n stat{
if P<1 {result:[lowest 1 of (2+(P*-1))d{(1+N)..(20+N)}]}
if P=1 {result: Pd{(1+N)..(20+N)}}
if P>1 {result:[highest 1 of Pd{(1+N)..(20+N)}]}
}
STAT:5 P:6 ARMOR:8 SL:6
\ATTACKING\
 
 ACTOR:[with P proficiency and STAT stat]
 DEFENSE:STAT+P+8
 ATTACK:[with P proficiency and STAT stat]>DEFENSE
 DAMAGE:[count {STAT+8+ARMOR..(20+STAT+8+ARMOR)} in [with P proficiency and STAT stat]]

\CASTING\
 output [with P proficiency and STAT stat]>12+SL
