\ https://rpg.stackexchange.com/q/198558: For a dice pool of 6d6(4+), where any dice showing 4 or 5 or 6 is a success, what is the anydice formula to have the probability of at least a double among the 4+ dice? \

function: any doubles in ROLL:s {
  loop X over ROLL {
    if (ROLL = X){
      result: 1
    }
  }
  result: 0
}


output [any doubles in 6d6]
