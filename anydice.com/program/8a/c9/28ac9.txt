\	SKILL10s + Lay Die	\

loop S over {1..9}
{
  F: (10-S)
  D: {1:S, 0:F}
  output SdD named "successes for skill[S] ([S] or lower on [S]d10)"
}

\ for full 100% scale on graph \ output 10   named "100% mark"
\ to show 33.3333333% on graph \ output d3+2 named "33.33333% line"

\	Notes - Goals:
1 success should equal an adequate level of accomplishment, representing a basic understanding or ability at the lowest level of a skill, eg. the level of a freshly matriculated journeyman; 1 success should happen most of the time, but not be guaranteed. Experts and Masters should be several standard deviations above that
