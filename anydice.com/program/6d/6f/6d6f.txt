\************************
EXPECTED DAMAGE PER SWING
Used to calculate expected damage per attack for D&D5e, taking misses and Critical hits into consideration.
Comparing non-magical weapons can be done by comparing Damage Per Hit, since you hit as often with both.
Magic weapons can help you hit a higher AC, so this tool will help you decide whether to use a +1 Dagger or a normal Shortsword.
This will also help you decide whether to take a bonus to-hit or a bonus to-damage.
Expected damage per swing is always dependent on your chance to hit, and therefore on the enemy's AC.
The tool will provide results for ACs over a range; use the range appropriate for the enemies.
************************\

ENEMY_AC_MIN: 10    \Enemy's AC, minumum to test\
ENEMY_AC_MAX: 10    \Enemy's AC, maximum to test\
PC_TO_HIT:    7     \Player's to-hit bonus (prof. bonus, ability mod, power attack, etc.)\
DAMAGE_DICE:  d4    \e.g. d4, d6, 2d6\
DAMAGE_FLAT:  5     \flat bonus from ability mod, magic weapon, duelist feature, etc.\

function: attack ROLL:n plus TO_HIT:n against AC:n for DICE plus FLAT{
 if ROLL = 20 {         \CRITICAL HIT (double dice)\
  result: DICE+DICE+FLAT
 }
 if ROLL = 1 {          \Crit fail (always misses)\
  result: 0
 }
 if ROLL+TO_HIT > AC {  \Hit!\
  result: DICE+FLAT
 }
 else {                 \Miss\
  result: 0
 }
}

output d4+1
output d6
output [attack d20 plus 8 against 10 for d4 plus 6] named "+1 Dagger"
output [attack d20 plus 7 against 10 for d4 plus 6] named "+1dmg Dagger"
output [attack d20 plus 7 against 10 for d6 plus 5] named "Shortsword"

loop N over {ENEMY_AC_MIN..ENEMY_AC_MAX} {
 output[attack d20 plus PC_TO_HIT against N for DAMAGE_DICE plus DAMAGE_FLAT] named "Expected Damage per Swing against AC[N]"
}
