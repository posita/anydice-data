\**************** EXPECTED DAMAGE PER SWING ***************\
\***********************************************************
* Used to calculate expected damage per attack for D&D5e,  * 
*  taking misses and Critical hits into consideration.     *
* Comparing non-magical weapons can be done by comparing   *
*  Damage Per Hit, since you hit as often with both.       *
* Magic weapons will help you hit a higher AC, so this tool*
*  can help you decide whether to use a +1 Dagger or a     *
*  non-magical Shortsword.                                 *
* This will also help you decide whether to take a bonus   *
*  to-hit or a bonus to-damage (e.g. Archery vs. Duelist). *
* Expected damage per swing is always dependent on your    *
*  chance to hit, and therefore on the enemy's AC.         *
* The tool will provide results for ACs over a range; use  *
*  an appropriate AC range for enemies you expect to fight.*
***********************************************************\
\**User Variables**\
ENEMY_AC_MIN: 10    \Enemy's AC, minimum to test\
ENEMY_AC_MAX: 10    \Enemy's AC, maximum to test\
PC_TO_HIT:     7    \Player's to-hit bonus (prof. bonus, ability mod, power attack, etc.)\
DAMAGE_DICE:  d4    \Weapon or spell damage, e.g. d4, d6, 2d6, 1d6+1d8 (Do NOT include flat damage)\
DAMAGE_FLAT:   5    \Flat damage from ability mod, magic weapon, duelist feature, etc.\

\**Function to roll dice and calculate damage**\
\**Champion fighters will need to modify Crit chance (replace "20" below)**\
function: attack ROLL:n plus TO_HIT:n against AC:n for DICE:d plus FLAT{
 if ROLL >= 20 {         \CRITICAL HIT (double dice)\
  result: DICE+DICE+FLAT
 }
 else if ROLL = 1 {          \Crit fail (always misses)\
  result: 0
 }
 else if ROLL+TO_HIT > AC {  \Hit!\
  result: DICE+FLAT
 }
 else {                 \Miss\
  result: 0
 }
}

loop AC over {ENEMY_AC_MIN..ENEMY_AC_MAX} {
 output[attack d20 plus PC_TO_HIT against AC for DAMAGE_DICE plus DAMAGE_FLAT] named "Expected Damage per Swing against AC[AC]"
}
