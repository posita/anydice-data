C:2d10

loop M over {0..4}{

I:d4+M
S:d6+M
R:[lowest of d8+M and 10]
L:[lowest of d10+M and 10]
E:[lowest of d12+M and 10]

output (I > 1@C) + (I > 2@C) named "Injury (d4) +[M]"
output (S > 1@C) + (S > 2@C) named "Stat (d6) +[M]"
output (R > 1@C) + (R > 2@C) named "Rare (d8) +[M]"
output (L > 1@C) + (L > 2@C) named "Legendary (d10) +[M]"
output (E > 1@C) + (E > 2@C) named "Exotic (d12) +[M]"
}
