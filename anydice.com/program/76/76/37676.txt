\ Weapon stats \
ATKS: 3
BS: 4
S: 6
AP: -1
D: 2

\ Enemy stats \
T: 6
SV: 6
WOUNDTHRESHOLD: 4
SAVETHRESHOLD: SV + AP


\ Rolls \
HITS: [count {BS..6} in ATKSd6]

WOUNDS: [count {WOUNDTHRESHOLD..6} in HITSd6]

SAVES:  [count {SV..6} in WOUNDSd6]

output HITS named "hits"
output WOUNDS named "wounds"
output (WOUNDS-SAVES)*D named "damage"
