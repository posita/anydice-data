\Highest Traveller characteristics under various rolling methods\

function: higher of ROLLA:s and ROLLB:s if total is at least NUM:n {
 if (ROLLA+ROLLB) > (NUM-1) { result: [highest of 1@ROLLA and 1@ROLLB] }
}

NROLL: 2d6
HROLL: [highest 2 of 3d6]
NCHAR: d(NROLL)
HCHAR: d(HROLL)
NSTATS: 3dNCHAR
HSTATS: 3dHCHAR

output 1@6dNCHAR named "Highest of 6 x 2d6"
output [highest of 1@NSTATS and 1@HSTATS] named "Highest of 3 x 2d6 and 3 x 3d6 drop lowest"
output [higher of NSTATS and HSTATS if total is at least 42] named "Highest of 3 x 2d6 and 3 x 3d6 drop lowest, with re-roll if total below 42"
output 1@6dHCHAR named "Highest of 6 x 3d6 drop lowest"
