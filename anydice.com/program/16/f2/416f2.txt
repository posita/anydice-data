The output is percent alive (value 0) and dead (value 1) after NUMBEROFMONTHS months at the specified bloodmark level.

This assumes 5 hits to kill a character, and all hits are healed between assassination attempts.

NUMBEROFMONTHS = the number of months of being at the bloodmark level. Can be any positive number
SCHEDULED = Chance any attempt will be scheduled in a month based on the bloodmark level. Can be 12,6,3,2,1
ATTEMPTSDIVISOR = Divisor for the number of attempts based on bloodmark level. Can be 1,2,3
SUCCESSCHANCE = the chance the assassination attempt is successful based on bloodmark level. Can be 20,10,5
NUMBEROFHITS = the number of hits inflicted per assassination attempt. Always 1d6
