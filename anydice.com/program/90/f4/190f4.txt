function: ROLL and DMG vs DODGE and ARMOR {
    if ROLL < DODGE {
        result: -1
    }
    if ROLL > ARMOR {
        result: DMG + 1
    }
    if ARMOR - ROLL < DMG {
        result: DMG - (ARMOR - ROLL)
    }
    else {
        result: 0
    }
}

output [attack at 5 accuracy 8 damage vs 14 dodge 15 armor] named "Damage dealt"

\* 
 * Meaning of results
 * 
 * Damage dealt is -1:
 *     The attack is dodged. No damage is dealt
 * 
 * Damage dealt is 0:
 *     The attack hits, but is completely absorbed by armor. No damage is dealt.
 * 
 * Damage dealt is between 1 and Base Damage:
 *     The attack is partially absorbed by armor. Target fills in X Lvl 1 Harm slots (X = Damage dealt)
 * 
 * Damage dealt is Base Damage + 1:
 *     The attack is a direct hit. Target takes 1 Harm of Lvl equal to the Damage dealt (Max harm lvl is 4)
 * 
 * Damage dealt is higher than Base Damage + 1:
 *     The attack is a critical hit. "Damage dealt" is set to the average damage for a critical hit.
 *     (Critical hit means you get a direct hit, then you immediately get a free attack roll)
