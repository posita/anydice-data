\You can freely adjust these values to see how they will affect your distributions.\
SIDES: 6
BONUS_MAX: 10

\Remove the back-slashes around the following line to see more than one die size at a time.\
\loop SIDES over {4,6} {\

  \You probably shouldn't adjust these values unless you *really* mean to.\
  TARGET_MAX: (SIDES - 1)
  POOL_MAX: (BONUS_MAX + TARGET_MAX)

  loop POOL over {1..POOL_MAX} {
    loop TARGET over {1..TARGET_MAX} {
      output [count {1..TARGET} in POOLdSIDES] named "[POOL]d[SIDES] vs [TARGET]"
    }
  }

\}\
\If you remove the slashes at the start of the loop, you need to remove the ones at the end, just above this line, as well.\
