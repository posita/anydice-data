\Calculate a KGS player's Max Damage\
function: kgs A:d and B:d {
 result: [explode [highest of A and B]]
}

A:d6
B:d10
\With Advantage\
C: [highest of [kgs A and B] and [kgs A and B]]
\Regular Roll\
D: [kgs A and B]
\With Disadvantage\
E: [lowest of [kgs A and B] and [kgs A and B]] 
output C + D + E named "Max Average Damage"
output C named "With Advantage"
output D named "Regular"
output E named "With Disadvantage"
