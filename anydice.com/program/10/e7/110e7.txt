DIE: 6
TN: 5
ATLEAST: 1

loop N over {1..10} {
  output Nd(1dDIE >= TN) >= ATLEAST named "[N]d6"
}

\ VIEW IN TRANSPOSED VIEW

N = number of dice
DIE: die face, 6 = d6
TN: target number, 5 = 5+
ATLEAST: how many you need, default 1

Results:
1: at least ATLEAST number of dice hitting the target number TN+
0: All dice were less than TN


So 8d6 has a 96.1% chance of hitting a 5 or a 6, and 3.9% chance to not\
