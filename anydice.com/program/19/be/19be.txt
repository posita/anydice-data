\Pathfinder Weapon Damage Calculator
created by Doug Strait 2012\

\This function calculates how much damage you do on an attack
ATTACKS is the number you add to your attack roll (e.g. 10), this can include iterative attacks (e.g. {6,1})
AC is the opponent's armor class (e.g. 17)
THREAT is your critical threat range (e.g. 19 if your threat is 19-20)
MULT is your critical multiplier (e.g. 2)
DAMAGE is your damage you deal with your weapon not including extra dice (e.g. 1d8+2)
EXTRA is extra damage dice from flaming weapon or sneak attack (e.g. 1d6) otherwise leave this blank\

function: ATTACKS:s vs AC:n crit THREAT:n MULT:n dmg DAMAGE:d EXTRA:d {
  SUM: {0}
  loop ATTACK over ATTACKS {
    SUM: SUM + [1d20 plus ATTACK ac AC crit THREAT MULT dmg DAMAGE EXTRA]
  }
  result: SUM
}
function: ATTACKS:s vs AC:n crit THREAT:n MULT:n dmg DAMAGE:d {
  result: [ATTACKS vs AC crit THREAT MULT dmg DAMAGE 0]
}
function: ROLL:n plus ATTACK:n ac AC:n crit THREAT:n MULT:n dmg DAMAGE:d EXTRA:d {
  if (ROLL = 20){ result: {MULT}d{DAMAGE} + EXTRA } 
  if ((ROLL >= THREAT) & (ROLL + ATTACK >= AC)) { result: {MULT}d{DAMAGE} + EXTRA }
  result: (ROLL + ATTACK >= AC) * (DAMAGE + EXTRA)
}

\Here are some examples:\
AC: 17
output [9       vs AC crit 19 2 dmg 2d6 + 13] named "great"
output [15      vs AC crit 19 2 dmg 3d6 + 20] named "great, rage, bull, enlarge"
output [{10,10} vs AC crit 20 2 dmg 1d6 + 10] named "claws, rage"
output [6       vs AC crit 20 2 dmg 1d6 + 4 ] named "javelin"
