\ the player rolls two ten sided dice (2d10 for short). The player wants one or both dice to roll less than or equal to a target number. \
TN: 5 \ Target number. If a die rolls equal or under this number, that die is counted as a success \
SUCCESS: 1d10 <= TN
output SUCCESS | SUCCESS named "TN = [TN] chance of at least one success"
output SUCCESS & SUCCESS named "TN = [TN] chance of exactly two successes"
output !SUCCESS & SUCCESS named "TN = [TN] chance of exactly 1 success"
output !SUCCESS & !SUCCESS named "TN = [TN] chance of complete failure"
