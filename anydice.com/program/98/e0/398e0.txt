function: swplode ROLL:n POWER:s CRIT:n XDMG:n{
 if ROLL=2 {result:0}
 if ROLL<CRIT {result: (ROLL-1)@POWER+XDMG}
 if ROLL>=CRIT {result: (ROLL-1)@POWER + [swplode 2d6 POWER CRIT 0] + XDMG}
}
set "maximum function depth" to 4

\How to output your damage roll chances\
\Change the 'TEN' to whatever you named your power table array at the top. Change '9' to your C-Value for the attack.\
\Change '5' to the attack's Extra Damage.\
