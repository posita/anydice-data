★ I think this makes sense, because adding dice means the character *could* exceed their normal range of successes, exceed their natural ability; thematically and realistically that does not make sense because that would be more like the helping person actually doing the work (and in the case of getting help from someone of lesser skill, that would make no sense at all. By keeping the same dice but shifting the skill/roll-under, that seems more like just getting a helping hand. I think the combined rolls of two or more characters - thus the potentially highly extended range - is more like a group effort.
