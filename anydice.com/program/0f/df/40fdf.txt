\ An example of rolling a d20 and adding a modifier as well as examples for rolling it twice and taking the highest or lowest \

loop I over {-3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3}{
 output 1d12 + I named "[I] modifier normally"
 output [highest 1 of 2d12] + I named "[I] modifier with Advantage"
 output [lowest 1 of 2d12] + I named "[I] modifier with Disadvantage"
}
