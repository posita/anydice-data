LEVEL: 5
DC: 25
CRIMINAL: 1d4
REWARD: 1000
COST: 25
STEALTH: 9
PERCEPTION: 9
TOOLS: 6

CS: d20 + STEALTH + CRIMINAL >= DC
CP: d20 + PERCEPTION + CRIMINAL >= DC
CT: d20 + TOOLS + CRIMINAL >= DC

ESC: CS | CP | CT
PARTIAL: (CS & CP) | (CS & CT) | (CP & CT)
SUCCESS: CS & CP & CT  

output ESC named "escape"
output PARTIAL named "partial success"
output (CS & CP & CT) named "success"

output ESC * 0 + PARTIAL * (REWARD/2)*LEVEL + SUCCESS * REWARD*LEVEL - COST named "expectation"
