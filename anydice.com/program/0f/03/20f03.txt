LEVEL: 5
DC: 20
CRIMINAL: 1d4
REWARD: 200
COST: 25
STEALTH: 9
PERCEPTION: 9
TOOLS: 6

RS: d20 + STEALTH + CRIMINAL
RP: d20 + PERCEPTION + CRIMINAL
RT: d20 + TOOLS + CRIMINAL

CS: RS >= DC
CP: RP >= DC
CT: RT >= DC

ESC: CS | CP | CT
PARTIAL: (CS & CP) | (CS & CT) | (CP & CT)
SUCCESS: CS & CP & CT  

output RS named "stealth roll"
output RP named "perception roll"
output RT named "tools roll"

output ESC named "escape"
output PARTIAL named "partial success"
output (CS & CP & CT) named "success"

output ESC * 0 + PARTIAL * (REWARD/2)*LEVEL + SUCCESS * REWARD*LEVEL - COST named "expectation"
