\Calculates the chance to hit of a monster spell\
HD: 27
EV: 10
\Spell Details:
You can find these in zap-data.h, in the monster_tohit field. They typically look like 
new tohit_calculator<FLATACCURACY, POWERNUMERATOR, POWERDENOMINATOR>. \
FLATACCURACY: 22
POWERNUMERATOR: 1
POWERDENOMINATOR: 20
\This is special-cased in monscast.cc::_mons_power_hd_factor() for a few spells; 12 is the default.\
SPELLPOWERMULT: 12 \Almost always 12\


output (1d(HD*SPELLPOWERMULT*POWERNUMERATOR/POWERDENOMINATOR+FLATACCURACY+1)-1) >= (1d(EV+1)-1 + 1d(EV+1)-1)/2 named "Just EV"

output (1d(HD*SPELLPOWERMULT*POWERNUMERATOR/POWERDENOMINATOR+FLATACCURACY+1)-1)*(1d101-1)/100 >= (1d(EV+1)-1 + 1d(EV+1)-1)/2 named "With Repel Missiles"

output (1d(HD*SPELLPOWERMULT*POWERNUMERATOR/POWERDENOMINATOR+FLATACCURACY+1)-1)*(1d101-1)/200 >= (1d(EV+1)-1 + 1d(EV+1)-1)/2 named "With Deflect Missiles"
