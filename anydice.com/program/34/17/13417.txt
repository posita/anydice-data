\Calculates the chance to hit of a monster spell\
HD: 27
EV: 10
SH: 10
NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT: 0
\Spell Details:
You can find these in zap-data.h, in the monster_tohit field. They typically look like 
new tohit_calculator<FLATACCURACY, POWERNUMERATOR, POWERDENOMINATOR>. \
FLATACCURACY: 22
POWERNUMERATOR: 1
POWERDENOMINATOR: 20
\This is special-cased in monscast.cc::_mons_power_hd_factor() for a few spells; 12 is the default.\
SPELLPOWERMULT: 12 \Almost always 12\

\Calculations:\

TOHIT: HD*SPELLPOWERMULT*POWERNUMERATOR/POWERDENOMINATOR+FLATACCURACY

SHTOHIT: TOHIT*13/10+4*NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT*NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT

EVROLL: (1d(EV+1)-1 + 1d(EV+1)-1)/2

SHBYPASS: 1d(SHTOHIT+1)-1 >= SH

EVBYPASS: (1d(TOHIT+1)-1) >= EVROLL

EVRMSLBYPASS: (1d(TOHIT+1)-1)*(1d101-1)/100 >= EVROLL

EVDMSLBYPASS: (1d(TOHIT+1)-1)*(1d101-1)/200 >= EVROLL

output SHBYPASS named "SH"

output [lowest of SHBYPASS and EVBYPASS] named "SH and EV"

output [lowest of SHBYPASS and EVRMSLBYPASS] named "SH and EV with Repel Missles"

output [lowest of SHBYPASS and EVDMSLBYPASS] named "SH and EV with Deflect Missles"
