\Number of dice\

\Maximum result on the dice\

\reroll on a result of REROLL or lower. Make sure it's lower than maximum result.\

ROLLA: d{(REROLL+1)..DICETYPE}
ROLLB: d{REROLL:REROLL, (REROLL+1)..DICETYPE}

output DICEAMTdDICETYPE named "Normal Roll"

output [highest DICEAMT of (2*DICEAMT)dDICETYPE] named "Advantage"
output DICEAMTdROLLA named "Reroll Always"
