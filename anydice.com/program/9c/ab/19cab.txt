function: ROLL:n attack  AC with BONUS deal DAMAGE extra CRITBONUSDAMAGE with critrange CRITRANGE {
   if(ROLL >= CRITRANGE){ \crit and autohit\
      result: DAMAGE + CRITBONUSDAMAGE 
   }
   if(ROLL + BONUS < AC) { result: 0 } \miss\
   result: DAMAGE \hit\
}
AC: 7

\LEVEL 6 FIGHTER VS. BASELINE without superiority with archery style\
HITBONUS: 3+4
output 2*[d20 attack AC with HITBONUS deal d10+4+d6 extra d10+d6 with critrange 20] named "Baseline vs AC [AC]"
output 2*[d20 attack AC with (HITBONUS-1)-10+2 deal 1d4+3+20 extra 1d4 with critrange 20] named "Fighter Wombo vs AC [AC] without Combat Superiority"

\LEVEL 8 RECKLESS BARBARION VS. BASELINE\
HITBONUS: 3+5
output 2*[d20 attack AC with HITBONUS deal d10+5+d6 extra d10+d6 with critrange 20] named "Baseline vs AC [AC]"
output 2*[[highest 1 of 2d20] attack AC with (HITBONUS-2)-10 deal 1d4+3+20 extra 1d4 with critrange 20] named "Barb Wombo vs AC [AC]"
