\ How to get the highest rolled values out of mixed dice. 

d12,d8,d6,d6 \

DTWELVE: {0,2..12}
DTEN: {0,2..10}
DEIGHT: {0,2..8}
DSIX: {0,2..6}

function: highest N:n of A:s and B:s { result: {1..N}@[sort {A, B}]}

CHAOS: [highest 2 of 2dDSIX and [highest 2 of 1dDEIGHT and 1dDTWELVE]]
WALKER:  [highest 2 of 2dDSIX and 6dDEIGHT] 
VS: WALKER >= CHAOS
output VS
output [count 0 in CHAOS] named "CHAOS Opportunity"
output [count 0 in WALKER] named "WALKER Opportunity"
