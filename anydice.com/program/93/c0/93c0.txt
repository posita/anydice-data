Alternative Dice Rolling for PbtA games, incorporating D&D5e style advantage and disadvantage.
    10- = bad
    11-15 = mixed
    16+ = good

Pros More granular than 2d6. Allows for easy and difficult rolls.
Cons requires d10s instead of d6's (not everyone has d10s). Slightly harder math.
