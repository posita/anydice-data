\ Roll pool of d10, see highest value. If 10, add +1 for each surplus 10. Compare against DC value, degree of success is difference in roll and DC \ 
function: degree of success DICE:s against dc DC:n {
 Y: 1@DICE
 X: [count {9,10} in DICE]-1
 if X<0{X:0}
 Z: X+Y
 result:Z-DC 
}
