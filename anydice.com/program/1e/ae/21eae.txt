\\\\\ Change these variables \\\\\
NUMBEROFDICE: 2    \ Number of dice you roll with a regular damage roll, e.g. "1" for a longsword (1d8 or 1d10), "2" for a greatsword (2d6). \
DIEFACES: 8        \ How many faces the damage die has, e.g. "8" for 1d8, "6" for 1d6 or 2d6. \
EXTRADICE: 0       \ Number of dice you roll *in addition* to the dice you get from a normal critical hit, e.g. Savage Attacker.\

\
* "Regular attack" is damage from a regular, non-critical hit.
* "RAW critical hit" is damage inflicted if you roll double the dice and use whatever result they land on, as described in the PHB.
* "At least half" means you roll double the dice, but round all individual dice up to at least half their maximum value. A 1, 2, or 3 on a d8 turns into a 4, for example.
* "Crit damage maximized" means all dice in addition to the regular damage dice are automatically maximized.
\

\\\\\ Main program \\\\\
output NUMBEROFDICEdDIEFACES named "Regular attack"
output (NUMBEROFDICE*2+EXTRADICE)dDIEFACES named "RAW critical hit"
output (NUMBEROFDICE*2+EXTRADICE)d[highest of DIEFACES/2 and 1dDIEFACES] named "At least half"
output NUMBEROFDICEdDIEFACES+NUMBEROFDICE*DIEFACES+EXTRADICE*DIEFACES named "Crit damage maximized"
