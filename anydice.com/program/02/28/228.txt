\ How to get the middle (or some other position) of mixed dice.
  The dice order does not matter.
  You can select a single position, like 2 for 2nd highest.
  You can also select multiple positions and they will be summed,
  like {1,2} for highest two, or {1,3} for 1st and 3rd highest.
 \

function: position P of A:s and B:s and C:s {
 result: P @ [sort {A, B, C}]
}

\ this second function isn't required,
  as [position 2 of d6 and d8 and {}] works,
  but it's more convenient \

function: position P of A:s and B:s {
 result: P @ [sort {A, B}]
}

output 2 @ 3d8 named "middle of 3d8"
output [position 2 of 2d8 and 1d6] named "middle of 2d8, d6"
output [position 2 of d6 and d8 and d12] named "middle of d12, d8, d6"
