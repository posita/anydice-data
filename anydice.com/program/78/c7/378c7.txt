function: damage D:n {
 if D=0 {result: 0}
 X: 0
 if D=2 {X:3d8}
 result: DdDICE+STAT+BONUS+X+4
}

STAT: 16
BONUS: 3
TOHIT: PROF+STAT+BONUS

MISS: [lowest of [highest of 1 and AC-TOHIT-1] and 18]
HIT: 18-MISS

DICE: 1d8
output 3d[damage [lowest 1 of 2d{0:MISS,1:HIT,2:2}] ]
DICE: 1d8+2d10+1d6
