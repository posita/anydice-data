POWER: 9
AC: 2
EV: 12

SANDBLASTTOHIT: 11+POWER/10

function: roll NUM:n dice of size SIZE:n {
  result: (NUM)d(SIZE)
}

EVROLL: [roll 2 dice of size ([roll 1 dice of size EV] - 1)]/2-1
TOHITROLL: 1dSANDBLASTTOHIT-1

SANDBLAST: [highest of 0 and 2d(4+POWER/3)-3d(AC+1)+3] 

output SANDBLAST named "sandblast, preEV"
output (TOHITROLL >= EVROLL)*SANDBLAST named "sandblast"
output 1d(3+POWER/5) named "freeze"
