function: dice X:n and Y:n of DICE:s  { 
  result: {(X@DICE + Y@DICE)}
}

function: dice W:n and X:n and Y:n and Z:n of DICE:s {
  result: {(W@DICE + X@DICE + Y@DICE + Z@DICE)}
}
