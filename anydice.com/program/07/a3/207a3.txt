\ this is kinda-sorta like a distribution of units' Defense stats \
D: 7+2d6

\ an archer has +K to attack twice at disadvantage vs. a missile cavalry \
\ a missile cavalry has +N to attack once vs. an archer\
loop K over {1..6}{
    loop N over {1..6}{
        output ([lowest 1 of 2d20]+K >= D) + ([lowest 1 of 2d20]+K >= D) - (1d20+N >= D) named "[K] vs [N]"
    }
}

\ result shows that high-attack archers > high-attack missile cav \
\ and the inverse as well, low-attack missile cav > low-attack archers \
