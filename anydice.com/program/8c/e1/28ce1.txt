\ This simulates the probability of winning a single task against another target (or resisting their actions). It is not perfect as it does not account for the probability of glitches. But these should be VERY rare for high dice pools. It also does not account for limits. \

MY_POOL: 17
THEIR_POOL: 17
EDGE_RATING: 5

X: MY_POOL d{0,0,1}
Y: THEIR_POOL d{0,0,1}

SECOND_CHANCE: (MY_POOL-X) d{0,0,1} + X    \ This simulates an Edge Reroll \
BREAK_LIMIT: (MY_POOL+EDGE_RATING) d[explode d{0,0,1}]

output X > Y named "No Edge Reroll"

output SECOND_CHANCE > Y named "Edge Reroll"

output BREAK_LIMIT > Y named "Breaking Limit"


\ This calculates the probability of hitting your limit given a roll type (reroll or regular). It will also give the possible successes for breaking the limit as that has no limit along with the chance that you will exceed your limit. \

MY_LIMIT: 9

output X > MY_LIMIT named "Chance Successes hits limit"
output SECOND_CHANCE > MY_LIMIT named "Chance Reroll Successes hits limit"
output BREAK_LIMIT > MY_LIMIT named "Chance that you will exceed your limit on a Break the Limit"
output BREAK_LIMIT named "Hits on a Break the Limit"
