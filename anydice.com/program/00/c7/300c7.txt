\ Calculate the number of die rolls between 1 and 6 that succeed or \
\ fail to meet the target; then define a relabeled die X that rolls \
\ 0 on a success and 1 on a failure. \
TARGET: 4
FAILURE: TARGET - 1
SUCCESS: 6 - FAILURE
X: {0:FAILURE, 1:SUCCESS}

\ Now roll N dice and count the number of successes. \
N: 10
output NdX named "[N]d6 vs [TARGET]"
output [highest N of (N+5)dX] named "([N]+5)d6 drop lowest 5 vs [TARGET]"  
output [lowest N of (N+5)dX] named "([N]+5)d6 drop highest 5 vs [TARGET]"
