function: reroll R:n under N:n {
   if R < N { result: 1d20 } else {result: R}
}

output [highest 1 of 2d20] + 10 named "levels 6 to 7, Power of Self"
output [reroll[highest 1 of 2d20] under 10] +1d4 +1d6 + 1d4 + 10 named "levels 6 to 8, w/ Power of Friendship"
output [highest 1 of 2d20] + 13 named "levels 9 to 12, Power of Self"
output [reroll[highest 1 of 2d20] under 10] +1d4 +1d10 + 1d4 + 13 named "levels 10 to 12, w/ Power of Friendship"

\power of self is just the advantage from raging.
The +13 at level 8+ assumes putting next ASI into STR (having one less in STR is more than fine if it doesn't happen)
power of friendship is: Chronal Shift (if rolled less than 10) + Emboldening bond + Guidance + Bardic Inspiration
at level 9 the only difference is bardic inspiration\
