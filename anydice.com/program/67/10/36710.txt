function: hit HIT:d crit CRIT:d atk DICE:n MOD:n ac AC:n res RES:n {
ATK: DICE+MOD
if (ATK>=AC+10)|(ATK>=AC & DICE=20) {result: CRIT-RES}
if (ATK>=AC)|(ATK>AC-10 & DICE=20) {result: HIT-RES}
result: 0
}

DMG_DIE: d10
HIT_FST: 2dDMG_DIE+4
CRIT_FST: (2dDMG_DIE+4)*2
HIT: DMG_DIE+4
CRIT: (DMG_DIE+4)*2
MOD: 9
AC: 18
RES: 0


output [hit HIT_FST crit CRIT_FST atk d20 MOD ac AC res RES]
output [hit HIT crit CRIT atk d20 MOD ac AC res RES]+[hit HIT crit CRIT atk d20 MOD-5 ac AC res RES]
