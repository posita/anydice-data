
\ Calculates the chances for simple D6 3e \
\ 1 = critical failure, -1 degrees of failure \
\ 2 = failure \
\ 3 = success \
\ 4 = success with 1 degree of success \
\ 5 = success with 2 degrees of success... etc \

function: DIED:d simpleroll {

X: [count 6 in DIED]
A: [highest of 0 and X-1 ]
Y: [highest 1 of DIED]

result: Y+A

}

output [ 3d6 simpleroll ]
