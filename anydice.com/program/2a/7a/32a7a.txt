\ An example of rolling a d20 and adding a modifier as well as examples for rolling it twice and taking the highest or lowest \

loop I over {11}{
 output 1d20 + I named "[I] modifier normally"
 output [highest 1 of 2d20] + I named "[I] modifier with Advantage"
 output [lowest 1 of 2d20] + I named "[I] modifier with Disadvantage"
}
