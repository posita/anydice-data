\ Hit/miss bonus/penalty function. Returns the half the difference from the AC to the attack roll\
function: HIT hmbp AC {
result: (HIT-AC)/2
}
\ To-hit roll with damage roll plus damage bonus vs AC function. Returns the damage result. 
If the To-hit is further than 1/4 of the AC below the AC, the damage is 0.
If the To-hit is less than 1/4 of the AC below the AC, the result is half the damage die.
If the To-hit is equal to or greater than the AC, the result is the damage die plus the damage bonus.\
function: HIT:n hit DAM dam plus DB vs AC {
if HIT >= AC {result: DAM + DB}
if HIT > (AC-(AC/4)) {result: DAM/2}
else{result: 0}
}
\ To-hit roll with damage roll plus damage bonus vs AC function. Returns the damage result. 
If the To-hit is further than 1/4 of the AC below the AC, the damage is 0.
If the To-hit is less than 1/4 of the AC below the AC, the result the damage die plus the Hit/miss Bonus/penalty (always negative here).
If the To-hit is equal to or greater than the AC, the result is the damage die plus the damage bonus plus the Hit/miss Bonus/penalty (always positive here).\
function: HIT:n hita DAM dam plus DB vs AC {
if HIT >= AC {result: DAM + DB + [HIT hmbp AC]}
if HIT > (AC-(AC/4)) {result: DAM/2 + [HIT hmbp AC]}
else{result: 0}
}
