\Calculates the chance to hit of a piercing monster spell. Accounts for WARLOCK MIRROR and EV.\
HD: 20
EV: 47
SH: 16
NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT: 0
\Spell Details:
You can find these in zap-data.h, in the monster_tohit field. They typically look like 
new tohit_calculator<FLATACCURACY, POWERNUMERATOR, POWERDENOMINATOR>. \
FLATACCURACY: 16
POWERNUMERATOR: 1
POWERDENOMINATOR: 25
\This is special-cased in monscast.cc::_mons_power_hd_factor() for a few spells; 12 is the default.\
SPELLPOWERMULT: 12 \Almost always 12\

\Calculations:\

TOHIT: HD*SPELLPOWERMULT*POWERNUMERATOR/POWERDENOMINATOR+FLATACCURACY

SHTOHIT: TOHIT*13/10+4*NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT*NUMBEROFBLOCKSSINCELASTINPUT

EVROLL: (1d(EV)-1 + 1d(EV)-1)/2

EVHIGHROLL: (1d(EV+5)-1 + 1d(EV+5)-1)/2

SHBYPASS: 1d(SHTOHIT)-1 >= SH

EVBYPASS: (1d(TOHIT)-1) >= EVHIGHROLL

EVRMSLBYPASS: (1d(TOHIT)-1)*(1d50+49)/100 >= EVROLL

output SHBYPASS named "WARLOCKS SHIELD BLOCK CHANCE"

output EVRMSLBYPASS named "EV with Repel Missles"

output EVBYPASS named "EV+5"

output [lowest of SHBYPASS and EVRMSLBYPASS] named "WARLOCKS SHIELD and EV with Repel Missles"

output [lowest of SHBYPASS and EVBYPASS ] named "WARLOCKS SHIELD and EV+5"
