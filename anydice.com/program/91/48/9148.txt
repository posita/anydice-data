\This looks at the concept where you roll a group of d20s and come up with a total success number based on a set difficulty.\

function: evaluate ROLL:s {
 
 CRIT: ROLL = 12
 SUCCESSES: ROLL >=10
 FAIL: ROLL <= 4 
 BOTCH: ROLL = 1

 GOOD: (SUCCESSES-CRIT) + (CRIT*2)
 BAD: (FAIL-BOTCH) + (BOTCH*2)

 result: GOOD - BAD
 
}

loop DICE over {1..7} {
 output [evaluate DICE d12] named "[DICE]d"
}
