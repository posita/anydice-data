\This looks at the concept of MTL saves, simply defining a success number and then seeing how many of those are achieved within a given
dice pool. This is really just a binomial distribution.\

function: evaluate ROLL:s {

 SUCCESSES: ROLL >=8
 
 result: SUCCESSES
 
}

loop DICE over {1..9} {
 output [evaluate DICE d12] named "[DICE]d"
}
