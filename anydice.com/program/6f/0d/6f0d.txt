\
 Hypothetical mechanic Silhoutte by Dreampod 9, but with d10:

 Roll Xd10, take the highest die.
 If it is 1-9, it is your result, otherwise, if it is 10,
 your result is 10 + 1 per each other die that shows ten.
\

function: silhouette ROLL:s{
 if 1@ROLL = 10 {
  result: 9 + [count 10 in ROLL]
 }
 result: 1@ROLL
}

loop D over {1..6}{
 output [silhouette Dd10] named "[D]d"
}
