\ How to get the highest rolled values out of mixed dice. \

function: highest N:n of A:s and B:s {
 result: {1..N}@[sort {A, B}]
}

output [highest 1 of 1d8]
output [highest 1 of 1d8 and 1d6]
output [highest 1 of 2d8]
output [highest 1 of 1d8 and 1d10]

output [highest 1 of 1d8 and 1d12]
output [highest 1 of 2d10]
output [highest 1 of 2d12]
output [highest 1 of 3d10]
output [highest 1 of 3d12]
