\ How would I notate a roll of 1-5 dice, choosing the top two to keep fully, and the bottom remainder to be halved (rounding down, so 0 is possible?). \

function: keep top N:n plus half remainder of ROLL:s {
 result: {1..N}@ROLL + {N+1..#ROLL}@ROLL / 2
}

loop N over {1..5} {
 output [keep top 2 plus half remainder of Nd6] named "[N]d"
}
