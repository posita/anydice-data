DICETYPE: 20
\Maximum result on the dice\

REROLL: 1
\reroll on a result of REROLL or lower. Make sure it's lower than maximum result.\

ROLLO: d{1..REROLL:REROLL, (REROLL+1)..DICETYPE:((DICETYPE*DICETYPE-REROLL*REROLL)/(DICETYPE-REROLL))}
ROLLA: d{(REROLL+1)..DICETYPE}

output dDICETYPE named "Normal Roll"
output ROLLO named "Reroll Once"
output ROLLA named "Reroll Always"
