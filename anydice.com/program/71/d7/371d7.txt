MINSKILL: 2
MAXSKILL: 6
DIFFICULTY: 3

\
 Result Interpretation:
 "0" is a catastrophe (0 dice were 4+ and at least 1 dice was a 1)
 "1" is a failure (0 dice were 4+)
 "2" is an incomplete success (at least 1 die was 4+, but total successes did not meet difficulty)
 "3" is a complete success (there were at least as many 4+ dice as difficulty)
 "4" is a triumph (there were at least as many 4+ dice as difficult and at least 1 was a 6)
\

function: parse ROLL:s vs DIFF{
 HITS: [count {4..6} in ROLL]
 ONES: [count {1} in ROLL]
 SIXES: [count {6} in ROLL]
 CARD: [count {1..6} in ROLL]

 if HITS >= DIFF & SIXES >= (CARD / 2)  {result: 4}
 else if HITS >= DIFF {result: 3}
 else if HITS > 0 {result: 2}
 else if HITS = 0 & ONES >= (CARD / 2) {result: 0}
 result: 1
}
