\ Z is the number of dice to roll \
Z: 20

\ D stores the dice rolled \
D: Zd4

\ E is similar to D, but uses a custom dice \
E: Zd{-5, -5, -5, 15}

\ A holds the count of losses in D \
A: [count {1,2,3} in D]

\ B holds the count of wins in D \
B: [count 4 in D]

\ We treat losses only as -5 and store it in L \
L: -5 * A

\ We treat all rolls as an initial -5 and store it in M \
M: -5 * Z

\ We treat wins as a net increase of 15 and store it in W \
W: 15 * B

\ We treat wins as a payout of 20 and store it in X \
X: 20 * B

\ Outputs can be summarized as follows then:
  L+W will give the distribution based off of the "net" calculations
  M+X will give the distribution based off of an entry fee and a payout instead
    E will give the distribution based off of the custom "net" dice \

output L+W named "Net Calculation"
output M+X named "Fee and Payout Calculation"
output E   named "Custom Dice Calculation"
