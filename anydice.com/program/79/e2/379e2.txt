function: does A:n hit B:n {
  if A < B { result: 1 }
  result: 20
}

AC: 20
STR_MOD: 4
PROF_BONUS: 5
ATTACK_MOD: STR_MOD + PROF_BONUS
GWM_ATTACK_MOD: ATTACK_MOD - 5

output [does 1d20+ATTACK_MOD hit AC] named "To Hit"
output [does 1d20+GWM_ATTACK_MOD hit 20] named "To Hit (GWM)"
output [does [highest 1 of 2d20]+ATTACK_MOD hit 20] named "To Hit (Reckless)"
output [does [highest 1 of 2d20]+GWM_ATTACK_MOD hit 20] named "To Hit (GWM+Reckless)"
