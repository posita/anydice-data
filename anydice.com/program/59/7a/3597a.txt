\Function for weapon/spell with attack rolls (Do not edit)\
function: ROLL:n vs AC:n {
 if ROLL >= CRIT_MIN  {
  result: CRIT_TOTAL
 }
 if ROLL + ATTACK_BONUS >= AC {
  result: HIT_TOTAL
 }
 result: 0
}

\Make changes here if you want to compare your attacks to enemies with higher ACs or saving throw bonuses\
AC: d{14..20}     \For each attack choose a random AC between 14 and 20\



\ Copy and edit the block below to compare additional attack options.\
ATTACKS: 2        \Number of Attacks per Round\
ATTACK_BONUS: 6   \Attack bonus to hit (Ability Modifier + Proficiency)\ 
DAMAGE_DICE: 1d6 + 2d6  \Damage dice on a standard hit (Include dice from both weapon and features like sneak attack)\
DAMAGE_BONUS: 4   \Damage bonus (Ability Modifier, 0 for most cantrips)\
CRIT_MIN: 20      \Minimum number required to score a critical hit (Champion Fighters should change to 18 or 19)\
CRIT_BONUS: 0     \Additional damage deal on a critical hit (e.g. Brutal Critical, Paladins choosing to smite on a crit)\
\Calculate the total damage for both a regular hit and a critical hit\
HIT_TOTAL: DAMAGE_DICE + DAMAGE_BONUS
CRIT_TOTAL: DAMAGE_DICE + DAMAGE_DICE + DAMAGE_BONUS + CRIT_BONUS
output  ATTACKS d[d20 vs AC] named "Daruth with FT"
