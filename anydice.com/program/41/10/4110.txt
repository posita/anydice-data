function: AA AD attacks DA DD {
 ATTACKROLL: d6
 DEFENCEROLL: d6
 ATTACKRESULT:AA+ATTACKROLL>DD+DEFENCEROLL
 DOCOUNTER:1-ATTACKRESULT
 ATTACKROLL: d6
 DEFENCEROLL: d6
 COUNTERATTACKRESULT:DA+ATTACKROLL>AD+DEFENCEROLL
 result: ATTACKRESULT-COUNTERATTACKRESULT*DOCOUNTER
}

loop AA over {1..5}{loop AD over {1..5}{loop DA over {1..5}{loop DD over {1..5}{
output [AA AD attacks 2 2] named "[AA]/[AD] attacks [DA]/[DD]"
}}}}
