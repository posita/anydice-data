MELEE: Kyll, Rilth, Thelonious, Namtoo
GUNS: Zephyr, Cayenne, Kip, Rahchet,
MAGIC: Buster, Cedric, Vervain (speculative)
#1: Zephyr
Friendly fire. What the fuck. Not to mention +11 to hit and higher damage than anyone else at all times. And the best AC and highest health. And infinite flight. You don't need to give him everything, you know... Or, maybe you do, cause he doesn't know how to add 3 and 3. Does poorly against high AC, but that's pretty much it.
#2: Kyll Gorr
5 punches per turn forever, with one being stunning strike. His only AoE move deals a pitiful 1d6 damage, but other than that, he should never lose a straight fight.
#3: Buster
Mediocre damage, poor survivability, great support. His damaging moves stun and set up advantage, spike growth can just stop a fight entirely, and haste is good all the time. The biggest problem is that he has to double or triple up on spells to keep pace with anybody else, so he burns through his resources fast.
#4: Cedric
Even as a pacifist, he can pull off a surprising amount of damage, and all his buffs get great mileage. The only thing really keeping him from being better is how limited his resources are... 8 spell points and 3 divinity uses.
#5: Rilrith
Below average damage, but great survivability. With rage and the wall, she reduces most common damage to below half. She has great support with bloodlust, as long as the team sticks close to her. Plus, with all her damage buffs, wicked bite will let her deal a great amount of damage once per day with a chance to frighten. Even still, buster could totally kick her ass if he wasn't dead.
#6: Rahchet
His gun is powerful, but he's not a good shot, and his spells are powerful, but he's not a good spellcaster. Web is really good, but it also kneecaps his own damage output, since any fire damage burns the webs away. Eldritch cannon is a good boost, but it takes a full action to set up, so it doesn't quite let him beat Rilrith.
#7: Cayenne
If she was level 5, she would be right above buster. She has the highest burst damage of anybody, but is also the most fragile. Her and Buster make a good team, but in isolation, she's not as great.
#8: Vervain
Alright man, hex is fuckin great. It will take her a turn to set it up, but its 1d6 damage per successful attack and 3 guaranteed damage per turn, which is like a kip's worth of damage. But, it does need concentration, and it only applies for her. At 5th level, her damage is better than Zephyr's, but this is judging her as she is now, and now, the only thing keeping her out of the bottom 3 is her utility with her charm effects and heroism.
#9: Namtoo Gorr
Namtoo has low damage, low health, and no extra effects. Frenzy and power attack should help mitigate this, but frenzy only takes him so far and power attack isn't worth it on average. He'd be pretty good in a vanilla campaign, still. Plus, this goes beyond judging him in isolation, but he's dumb as a box of rocks and almost got killed by piranhas. If he had the fullblade, he could do more damage than rilrith, but he still wouldn't be as good as her. Rilrith has chants, and wicked bite, and combat superiority for some reason, and the combat superiority is better than it should be, and... yeah, man, artemis gets carried away with the npc lackeys.
#10: Thelonious
He does better damage than you expect, and every hit can knock the enemy prone, which is fantastic for support. The only problem is his abysmal 20 hit points, but, buster can just re-summon him whenever he gets sneezed on. I have no clue how this is supposed to be balanced in normal play. Bitch is just a whole extra character... Judging him in isolation, though, he dies too fast. If he was important, we'd stuff him into Zephyr so he wouldnt die.
#11: Kip
Kip has one REALLY good combo, with cunning action + spring attack giving him a totally free, damage boosted attack four times a day, but since Artemis is the one controlling him, he'll never get to do this. Outside of that, he does the worst damage and has low health, and is only really good for being Cedric's character arc. If he used spring attack, he might be up at #8, but artemis doesnt think things through so instead he'll have to settle for being the strongest character in any vanilla dnd campaign.
