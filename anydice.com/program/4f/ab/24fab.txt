\ How to get the highest rolled values out of mixed dice. \

function: highest N:n of A:s and B:s and C:s and D:s {
 result: {1..N}@[sort {A, B, C, D}]
}

output [highest 2 of 1d12 and 1d12 and 1d10 and 1d4]
output [highest 2 of 1d12 and 1d12 and 1d10 and 1d6]
output [highest 2 of 1d12 and 1d12 and 1d10 and 1d8]
output [highest 2 of 1d12 and 1d12 and 1d10 and 1d10]
output [highest 2 of 1d12 and 1d12 and 1d10 and 1d12]
