AWA: 10
EA: 10
AC: -1
DC: 0
PACC: ACC+AWA+10*(2*AC+1)
PED: (1+DC)d10+(DC+1)*10+(EA-10)
if EA <= 0 {

}
output PACC >= PED named "Conditional Hit Rate: [AWA] Awareness vs. [EA] Alacrity"
